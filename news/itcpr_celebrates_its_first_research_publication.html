ITCPR Celebrates Its First Research Publication

February 9, 2024
By ITCPR Team

We are thrilled to announce a major milestone for ITCPR — our first research paper has been published!

What began as a casual conversation in January 2024 has grown into a thriving initiative dedicated to supporting emerging researchers. ITCPR, though still an unofficial group, has taken its first concrete step toward becoming a hub for innovation in plasmonics and nanophotonics.

This groundbreaking achievement comes thanks to the incredible efforts of Tonmoy Malaker, Nourin Nurain Amina, and Md Faiyaz Bin Hassan. With guidance, mentorship, and sheer determination, this team transformed an idea into impactful research — culminating in the publication of their paper in a peer-reviewed journal.

Read the Paper: https://doi.org/10.1007/s11468-025-02817-8

Though ITCPR isn't officially registered yet, our name proudly appears in the acknowledgment section. It's a symbolic beginning — a promise of what's to come. We aim to open doors for students and early-career researchers who may not always have access to traditional academic pathways.

To celebrate this accomplishment and inspire further curiosity, we invite you to a public talk exploring the themes of our research:

Event Details:

  • Title: Plasmonic Nanostructures: A New Era in Nanophotonics
  • Speaker: Nourin Nurain Amina
  • Date: Sunday, February 9, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 PM (GMT+6)
  • Location: Join via Google Meet

This event is open to all — students, researchers, and tech enthusiasts alike — and will delve into the future potential of nanophotonics, quantum optics, and plasmonic technologies.

Let's celebrate this exciting chapter in ITCPR's journey and spark new discussions in the world of advanced physics. This is just the beginning!
