Crossing Continents for Education: A Bangladeshi's Pursuit of U.S. Graduate Degree

Posted: DATE
Written by: Md Abdus Sami Akanda
Studying for a graduate degree in the United States can be an exciting and rewarding experience, but it can also be daunting, especially for students from Bangladesh. The application process, visa requirements, and cultural adjustments can be challenging to navigate.

Hello, my name is Md Abdus Sami Akanda. Conquered the academic frontier? Not quite! But as a Bangladeshi PhD student in Physics at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, I'm here to share my hard-earned lessons and practical tips to help you navigate the US graduate school system and claim your own academic victory.

Let's now unpack this in detail, one step at a time.

Charting Your Course

Identify your field of passion: So, what really gets your neurons firing? That's your PhD sweet spot! Forget just the broad BSc/MSc stuff that you completed, pick a niche subfield like Solid State Physics, Spintronics, Photonics, or Nuclear Physics! Not sure yet? No sweat, apply anyway! Professors like excited explorers, and your passion for specific topic can make your application stand out. Remember, the academic ocean is vast, so your interests can shift as you sail.

Research universities and programs: Got your academic compass pointing north? Time to find the PhD program that ignites your research fire! Imagine a jungle of universities, each brimming with programs tailor-made for specific interests. Top Universities can be your map, showing you the hot spots for research and rankings, while ChatGPT helps you compare the vibe, location, and financial opportunities. But don't just follow the map - chat with fellow students in grad school with similar path to your interest, they have the inside scoop on the coolest professors and hidden gems. Ultimately, trust your gut! Choose the program that makes your neurons dance and feels like the perfect fit for your academic adventure.

Create a Google Sheet: Forget juggling a million tabs - organize your PhD quest with one master spreadsheet! Track universities, programs, deadlines, and more, all neatly lined up in your Google Sheet. This ain't no pre-made template, this is your personalized PhD command center. Here is my template: University List - Template. Copy it, customize it, and conquer the application process like a boss. Let's make your academic adventure organized AF.

Preparing for Standardized Tests

Conquer the GRE: Before you unleash your GRE fury on your dream program's application pool, check your armor. A stellar score can be your titanium shield, but make sure they even require the test first! Don't waste brainpower on pointless fights. This video from Fermion Physics Club, Guidelines on GRE, is your intel. Now, sharpen your pencils, fuel your caffeine engines, and crush that GRE like the academic predator you are! Remember, a strong score isn't just a number, it's a weapon in your arsenal.

Master TOEFL or IELTS: Conquering your dream program requires an English proficiency certificate, and you get to choose your between IELTS or TOEFL, depending on the requirements. Research the program's entry criteria, then train your language muscles at home. Mock exams at a test center are your bootcamp, but if you're a linguistic ninja, go solo! Fermion Physics Club has your intel for IELTS: Guidelines on IELTS & TOEFL: Guidelines on TOEFL. Crush those tests and prove your academic fluency.

Plan your test dates: Strategically schedule your IELTS or TOEFL exam, aligning it with program deadlines. Procrastination is the serpent in your academic garden - slay it with decisive action!

Gathering Your Academic Credentials

Obtain official transcripts: Obtain official transcripts from every institution you've traversed, starting from your bachelor degree. No dusty drawers - scan or cloud those transcripts for instant access, especially for the applications. Your transcripts are your academic map, showcasing your journey and unlocking doors to program paradise.

Request an endorsed copy: Ensure your transcripts carry the official endorsement and seal from the administrative staff; these are essential for university admission, with some institutions requiring them during the application process. You may need to personally deliver them to your university or use courier services such as DHL Express or FedEx for submission.

Securing Strong Recommendations

Choose professors who know you well: When choosing professors for recommendation letters, prioritize individuals who can offer profound insights into your academic capabilities and future potential. Opt for educators who are not only acquainted with your coursework but also have a keen understanding of your intellectual strengths, personal growth, and aspirations. Their recommendations should reflect a comprehensive perspective on your academic journey, enriching your application with authentic and compelling endorsements.

Draft a template (optional): Provide a succinct template outlining your academic achievements and research interests to assist your recommenders. Summarize key milestones and projects, ensuring clarity on your areas of study. This guide serves as a roadmap for recommenders to craft letters that align with your goals, enhancing the authenticity and relevance of their endorsements.

Request letters well in advance: Ensure your recommenders have the time required to craft meaningful and impactful letters that truly highlight your strengths and accomplishments. This investment in time allows for a more thorough exploration of your qualifications, enhancing the authenticity and effectiveness of their recommendations.

Provide your CV, SOP, and course information Equip your recommenders with essential materials such as your CV, Statement of Purpose (SOP), and course details to empower them in composing robust recommendation letters. Sharing this pertinent information ensures that they have a comprehensive understanding of your academic and professional background, enabling them to write compelling and tailored letters of recommendation.

Crafting a Compelling Curriculum Vitae and Statement of Purpose

Curriculum Vitae


Be authentic and showcase your unique skills and experiences. Avoid relying on AI-generated content.

Highlight relevant academic achievements, research projects, and extracurricular activities.

Tailor your SOP to each program: Explain why you are a perfect fit for the specific program you are applying to.

Create a simple, professional CV: Highlight your education, awards, work experience, and skills relevant to your field of study.

Navigating the Application Process

Fill out applications online: Carefully follow the instructions provided by each university.

Upload all required documents: Ensure you submit all necessary documents, including your transcripts, test scores, SOP, CV, and recommendation letters.

Pay application fees: Explore waiver options if available.

Bracing Yourself for the Selection Process and Interviews

Stay patient: The selection process can take several months, so be patient and persistent.

Prepare for potential interviews: Research the program and be ready to discuss your academic background, research interests, and career goals.

Practice presenting your research: Prepare a clear and concise presentation of your research to impress the interviewers.

Securing Your Finances

Accept your offer letter and request an I-20 form: This form is crucial for obtaining your student visa.

Connect with other Bangladeshis in the US for support and guidance: Seek advice and build a network of Bangladeshi students in your chosen city.

Conquering the Student Visa (F-1 Visa)

Create an account on CGI Federal and fill up the DS-160 form.

Pay the visa appointment fee and SEVIS fee.

Request an emergency slot if needed: If your travel dates are approaching soon, you can request an emergency visa appointment.

Preparing for Your Departure

Make a checklist of essentials to carry: Pack important documents, clothing, and personal items.

Connect with Bangladeshi students in your chosen city: Seek recommendations for accommodation, transportation, and local resources.

Invest in good luggage and suitable clothing for the US climate: Pack sturdy luggage and appropriate clothing for the weather conditions in your chosen city.

Research accommodation options close to your university: Find safe and convenient housing near your campus.

Set up a Walmart account for convenient shopping: Simplify your shopping experience by setting up an account with a major retailer like Walmart.

Remember, this guide is just the beginning. Your journey will be filled with challenges and triumphs
