Research Collaborations

ITCPR welcomes collaborative partnerships with academic institutions, research organizations, and individual researchers worldwide. Together, we can push the boundaries of theoretical and computational physics and create meaningful scientific impact.

Types of Collaborations

Research Collaborations

Collaborative research initiatives, co-authored publications, and shared computational resources for advancing scientific knowledge.

Steps to Collaborate with ITCPR

  1. Identify Research Interests: Define your research interests and how they align with ITCPR's focus areas.
  2. Contact Us: Reach out via email to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.
  3. Proposal Submission: Submit a detailed research proposal outlining objectives, methodologies, and expected outcomes.
  4. Review and Agreement: Our team will review your proposal and discuss terms of collaboration.
  5. Initiate Collaboration: Upon agreement, we will initiate the collaboration and outline the next steps.

Research Proposal Guidelines

We encourage researchers and institutions to submit collaboration proposals. Please follow our guidelines to ensure your proposal receives proper consideration.
